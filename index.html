Tanaka Dhombo

Venture Builder · Operator · Recovering Lawyer

I spent four years at one of Africa's top law firms executing cross-border deals across M&A, private equity, and venture capital. In doing so, I honed my ability to stress-test structures, align stakeholders, and making sure nothing falls apart the night before signing, save for my sleep schedule.

Now I build. I'm an MBA candidate at HEC Paris, MBA Council President, and experimenting with ventures at the intersection of AI, creator finance, and social connection. I'm drawn to early-stage ecosystems, search funds, and the operators who make things move on the continent.

I believe Africa's next chapter gets written by people who understand both the boardroom and the build, and I intend to be one of them.
